About Us

The Chhatrapati Shahu Maharaj Shikshan Sanstha (C.S.M.S.S.) was established in 1986 and was named after a renowned king and social worker of the State - CHHATRAPATI SHAHU MAHARAJ. The Young Enthusiastic & Dynamic engineer Mr. Ranjeet Padmakar Mulay is the President of the Trust. The renowned social worker, industrialist of the region and National Award Winner Mr. Padmakar Haribhau Mulay is the Secretary of the Trust and Shri. Vitthal Pandurang Lahane (Mama) is the Treasurer of the trust.

The CSMSS CHH. SHAHU COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING (DTE Code: 2533), Kanchanwadi, Paithan Road, Aurangabad (MS) is located within Municipal Corporation area of Aurangabad. It is a pollution free, secure, safe and peaceful locality. It is located in the educational environment having various colleges such as Dental, Ayurved, Agriculture, Polytechnic and Engineering and is just 2 kms from Aurangabad Railway Station and 4 kms from Central Bus Stand. Aurangabad is a historical and a rich cultural centre located 375 kms. east of Mumbai and is very well connected by road, rail and air. The world heritage sites of Ajanta and Ellora Caves are located around Aurangabad.

Hostel Facility

The CSMSS offers hostel facility located within the College campus. We understand that most of our students are moving away from home fot the ﬁist time, so we strive to mate the transition as smooth as possible by offering comfortable and safe hostels with a homely atmosphere. College offers seperate hostels for boys and girls with hostel warden in each hostel to supervise day to day management, in addition to this round the clock security is provided to the hostels. All the rooms have been designed and furnished to provide adequate moving space and ample air & Light. Canteen facility is attached to the hostels. The girl’s hostel has a capacity of 600 seats and boys’ hostel has a capacity of 100 seats. It is the endeavor of the management to provide hostel facility to needy students. However, hostel accommodation cannot be guaranteed and depends upon availabiity.
